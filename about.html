About Us

Welcome to the Lourdes Matha College of Science and Technology (LMCST) Canteen, a vibrant and welcoming space where students, faculty, and visitors can enjoy delicious, nutritious, and affordable meals. Our canteen is more than just a place to eat—it’s a hub for relaxation, conversation, and refreshment, offering a comfortable dining experience for everyone on campus.

About Lourdes Matha College of Science and Technology (LMCST)

Lourdes Matha College of Science and Technology, established in 2002, is a premier institution committed to excellence in education, innovation, and research. Located in Kuttichal, Thiruvananthapuram, LMCST is affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) and approved by the AICTE (All India Council for Technical Education). The college is known for its academic rigor, state-of-the-art facilities, and a student-centric approach to learning.

LMCST offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various fields of engineering, technology, and management, fostering an environment that encourages creativity, critical thinking, and professional growth. With a strong emphasis on technical education and holistic development, the college has gained a reputation as one of the leading institutions in Kerala.
